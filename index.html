Auction Price Estimation Methodology
Estimated auction prices are based on analysis of 45 non-timeshare property sales from the 2024–2025 SD County tax auctions (Tax Sales 7091, 7091B, 7095, 7095B). Historical data shows properties sold at 1.5x–26x their minimum bid, with the median at 4.6x. Multipliers are adjusted for property type (improved vs land), location (coastal premium, remote discount), subtype (condo vs SFH), and risk flags. Tax deed properties typically sell at 30–60% of market value. These are estimates only — actual auction prices depend on bidder competition, property condition, and market conditions.
